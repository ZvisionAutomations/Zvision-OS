# Strategic Frameworks Reference: Strategic Council

## Purpose
Referencia consolidada dos frameworks estrategicos dos 10 conselheiros. Guia rapido para identificar qual framework aplicar em cada situacao.

---

## Ray Dalio — Frameworks

### 1. Principles Meta-Framework
- **Use When**: Precisa criar regras sistematicas para decisoes recorrentes
- **Core**: Values → Principles → Actions
- **Key Output**: Principios escritos que conectam valores a acoes

### 2. 5-Step Process
- **Use When**: Precisa resolver qualquer problema de forma estruturada
- **Core**: Goals → Problems → Diagnosis → Design → Execute
- **Key Rule**: Nunca pular etapas ou misturar etapas

### 3. Pain + Reflection = Progress
- **Use When**: Apos uma falha ou experiencia dolorosa
- **Core**: Dor + Reflexao honesta = Novo principio
- **Key Output**: Principio que previne recorrencia

### 4. Idea Meritocracy
- **Use When**: Precisa melhorar qualidade de decisoes em grupo
- **Core**: Radical Truth + Radical Transparency + Believability-Weighted Decisions
- **Tools**: Dot Collector, Baseball Cards, The Combinator

### 5. Economic Machine Model
- **Use When**: Analisando ciclos economicos, mercados, ou alocacao macro
- **Core**: Productivity + Short-Term Debt Cycle + Long-Term Debt Cycle
- **Key Insight**: True diversification = risk parity, not dollar parity

### 6. All Weather Strategy
- **Use When**: Construindo portfolio resiliente a todos os cenarios
- **Core**: Balance risk across 4 environments (growth/inflation x rising/falling)

## Charlie Munger — Frameworks

### 7. Latticework of Mental Models
- **Use When**: Precisa analisar um problema de multiplas perspectivas
- **Core**: Aplicar 3+ modelos de disciplinas diferentes simultaneamente
- **Key Rule**: "Man with a hammer" syndrome — evite modelo unico

### 8. Inversion
- **Use When**: Qualquer decisao importante
- **Core**: "What would guarantee failure?" → Avoid those things
- **Key Phrase**: "All I want to know is where I'm going to die, so I'll never go there"

### 9. Circle of Competence
- **Use When**: Avaliando se voce (ou equipe) tem expertise para decidir
- **Core**: Know the edge of your knowledge. Stay inside for important decisions.

### 10. Checklist Methodology
- **Use When**: Antes de qualquer decisao de alto impacto
- **Core**: Systematic pre-decision checklist of failure modes and biases

### 11. Moat Analysis
- **Use When**: Avaliando vantagem competitiva de um negocio
- **Core**: Brand, Network Effects, Switching Costs, Cost Advantages, Intangibles
- **Key Question**: "Can a well-funded competitor replicate this in 3 years?"

### 12. 25 Cognitive Biases
- **Use When**: Suspeitando que vieses estao distorcendo julgamento
- **Core**: Systematic catalog of human misjudgment tendencies

## Naval Ravikant — Frameworks

### 13. Wealth Creation Formula
- **Use When**: Construindo estrategia de criacao de riqueza
- **Core**: Specific Knowledge x Leverage x Accountability = Wealth
- **Key Insight**: Permissionless leverage (code, media) is the great equalizer

### 14. Productize Yourself
- **Use When**: Transformando habilidades em produto escalavel
- **Core**: Find specific knowledge → Choose leverage → Take accountability

### 15. Happiness as a Skill
- **Use When**: Buscando paz e satisfacao alem do sucesso material
- **Core**: Peace (absence of desire) + Choice + Presence

### 16. Judgment Framework
- **Use When**: Melhorando qualidade de decisoes
- **Core**: Clear thinking + Long-term games + First principles

### 17. Compounding Life
- **Use When**: Planejando para o longo prazo
- **Core**: Knowledge, relationships, reputation, health, skills — all compound

## Peter Thiel — Frameworks

### 18. Zero to One
- **Use When**: Avaliando se uma ideia e genuinamente inovadora
- **Core**: Creating new (0→1) vs copying (1→n)
- **Key Question**: "What important truth do very few people agree with you on?"

### 19. Seven Questions
- **Use When**: Avaliando viabilidade de um negocio
- **Core**: Engineering, Timing, Monopoly, People, Distribution, Durability, Secret

### 20. Competition Is For Losers
- **Use When**: Definindo estrategia competitiva
- **Core**: Build monopoly, don't compete. Start small, dominate, expand.

### 21. Power Law
- **Use When**: Alocando recursos ou investimentos
- **Core**: A small number of bets generate the vast majority of returns

### 22. Definite Optimism
- **Use When**: Planejando o futuro
- **Core**: Have specific plans, not vague hopes. Engineers > financiers.

### 23. Mimetic Theory (Girard)
- **Use When**: Questionando motivacoes e desejos
- **Core**: Most desires are borrowed. Competition is mimetic rivalry.

## Reid Hoffman — Frameworks

### 24. Blitzscaling
- **Use When**: Decidindo velocidade de crescimento
- **Core**: Speed > efficiency in winner-take-most markets with network effects
- **5 Stages**: Family → Tribe → Village → City → Nation

### 25. Network Effects Taxonomy
- **Use When**: Avaliando defensibilidade de um modelo de negocio
- **Core**: Direct, Indirect (Two-Sided), Data, Platform

### 26. ABZ Planning
- **Use When**: Planejando carreira ou estrategia de negocio
- **Core**: Plan A (current), Plan B (adjacent pivot), Plan Z (lifeboat)

### 27. The Alliance
- **Use When**: Desenhando relacao empregador-empregado
- **Core**: Tours of Duty (Rotational, Transformational, Foundational)

### 28. Permanent Beta
- **Use When**: Qualquer momento — mentalidade continua
- **Core**: Never finished. Always iterating. Launch before ready.

## Simon Sinek — Frameworks

### 29. Golden Circle (Start With Why)
- **Use When**: Comunicando, liderando, ou posicionando marca
- **Core**: WHY (purpose) → HOW (values) → WHAT (products)
- **Law**: People don't buy what you do, they buy why you do it

### 30. Infinite Game
- **Use When**: Definindo estrategia de longo prazo
- **5 Practices**: Just Cause, Trusting Teams, Worthy Rivals, Existential Flexibility, Courage to Lead

### 31. Leaders Eat Last
- **Use When**: Construindo cultura e confianca
- **Core**: Circle of Safety. Leaders sacrifice comfort for those in their charge.

### 32. Trust vs Performance Matrix
- **Use When**: Avaliando membros de equipe
- **Key Insight**: Low-trust, high-performance people are the MOST toxic

## Brene Brown — Frameworks

### 33. Dare to Lead (4 Skill Sets)
- **Use When**: Desenvolvendo lideranca corajosa
- **Core**: Rumbling with Vulnerability, Living into Values, BRAVING Trust, Learning to Rise

### 34. BRAVING Trust Inventory
- **Use When**: Avaliando e construindo confianca
- **Core**: Boundaries, Reliability, Accountability, Vault, Integrity, Non-judgment, Generosity

### 35. Shame Resilience Theory
- **Use When**: Lidando com medo de falha ou vergonha
- **Core**: Shame vs Guilt. Shame dies when spoken in safe places.

### 36. Rising Strong Process
- **Use When**: Recuperando de uma queda ou falha
- **Core**: Reckoning → Rumble → Revolution

### 37. Empathy vs Sympathy
- **Use When**: Respondendo a alguem em dificuldade
- **Core**: Empathy = feeling WITH. Sympathy = feeling FOR (from above).

## Patrick Lencioni — Frameworks

### 38. Five Dysfunctions of a Team
- **Use When**: Diagnosticando por que uma equipe nao funciona
- **Pyramid**: Trust → Conflict → Commitment → Accountability → Results

### 39. The Advantage (Org Health)
- **Use When**: Melhorando saude organizacional
- **4 Disciplines**: Cohesive Leadership, Create Clarity, Over-Communicate, Reinforce

### 40. Six Types of Working Genius
- **Use When**: Entendendo por que pessoas estao frustradas no trabalho
- **Types**: Wonder, Invention, Discernment, Galvanizing, Enablement, Tenacity

### 41. Ideal Team Player
- **Use When**: Contratando ou avaliando membros de equipe
- **Core**: Humble + Hungry + Smart. Never compromise on humble.

### 42. Death by Meeting (4 Types)
- **Use When**: Redesenhando cadencia de reunioes
- **Types**: Daily Check-In, Weekly Tactical, Monthly Strategic, Quarterly Off-Site

## Derek Sivers — Frameworks

### 43. Hell Yeah or No
- **Use When**: Filtrando oportunidades e compromissos
- **Core**: If it's not a clear "HELL YEAH!" then it's a no

### 44. Ideas x Execution
- **Use When**: Avaliando uma ideia de negocio
- **Core**: Ideas are multipliers ($-1 to $20). Execution is the value ($1 to $10M).

### 45. Useful Not Famous
- **Use When**: Definindo estrategia de posicionamento pessoal
- **Core**: Help deeply rather than reach broadly

### 46. Stay Small on Purpose
- **Use When**: Questionando pressao para crescer
- **Core**: Bigger is not better. Define enough. Stop when you get there.

---

## Yvon Chouinard — Frameworks

### 47. Let My People Go Surfing
- **Use When**: Desenhando cultura e gestao de equipe
- **Core**: Hire passionate people, give freedom, trust them, mission first

### 48. Responsible Company (4 Stakeholders)
- **Use When**: Avaliando impacto holístico de um negocio
- **Core**: Workers + Customers + Community + Nature

### 49. Don't Buy This Jacket
- **Use When**: Questionando consumismo e planned obsolescence
- **Core**: Build to last. Repair before replace. The best product is the one you already own.

### 50. Earth as Shareholder
- **Use When**: Pensando em proposito final de um negocio
- **Core**: Business exists to serve a purpose larger than profit

## Framework Selection Guide

| Situacao | Framework Recomendado | Advisor |
|----------|----------------------|---------|
| Decisao complexa com dados | 5-Step Process + Expected Value | Dalio |
| Avaliando negocio/investimento | Moat Analysis + Inversion | Munger |
| Criando produto/servico | Productize Yourself + Leverage | Naval |
| Avaliando ideia de startup | Seven Questions + Zero to One | Thiel |
| Decidindo quando/como crescer | Blitzscaling + Network Effects | Hoffman |
| Articulando proposito | Golden Circle + Just Cause | Sinek |
| Construindo confianca | BRAVING + Dare to Lead | Brown |
| Consertando equipe disfuncional | Five Dysfunctions + Working Genius | Lencioni |
| Simplificando decisoes | Hell Yeah or No + Enough | Sivers |
| Alinhando missao e impacto | Responsible Company + 4 Stakeholders | Chouinard |
