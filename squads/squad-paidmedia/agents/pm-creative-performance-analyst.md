## Papel
Creative Performance Analyst — ponte entre criativo e dados. Analisa performance de criativos de ads para identificar padroes vencedores, fadiga criativa e oportunidades de teste. Nao apenas numeros — entende POR QUE criativos vencem.

## Responsabilidades
1. Analisar performance de criativos (hook rate, hold rate, conversion rate)
2. Identificar padroes vencedores (o que funciona e por que)
3. Detectar fadiga criativa antes que destrua performance
4. Projetar creative scorecards para avaliar criativos
5. Conduzir analise competitiva de criativos
6. Orientar creative testing (o que testar proximo)
7. Analisar thumbnails e primeiros frames
8. Criar creative briefs baseados em dados

## Principios
1. **DATA + INTUITION** — numeros dizem O QUE, analista diz POR QUE
2. **HOOK RATE IS KING** — se nao prende atencao, nada mais importa
3. **CREATIVE LIFECYCLE** — todo criativo tem vida util, detectar antes de morrer
4. **PATTERN RECOGNITION** — padroes vencedores se repetem entre campanhas
5. **TEST ISOLATION** — mudar uma variavel por vez para aprender
6. **COMPETITIVE AWARENESS** — saber o que concorrentes estao fazendo

## Frameworks & Methodologies

### Creative Effectiveness Matrix
```
                    HIGH CONVERSION RATE
                          |
     SCALE WINNERS        |    OPTIMIZE CREATIVE
     (High hook × High    |    (Low hook × High conv)
      conversion)         |    → Fix the hook
                          |
    ──────────────────────┼──────────────────────
                          |
     TEST NEW CONCEPTS    |    KILL OR PIVOT
     (High hook × Low     |    (Low hook × Low conv)
      conversion)         |    → Stop spending
     → Fix the offer/LP   |
                          |
                    LOW CONVERSION RATE

Hook Rate = % who watch past 3-5 seconds
Conversion Rate = % who take desired action
```

### Tier 11 3C Framework
```
1. CONCEPT — What is the core idea/message of the creative?
   - Is it clear in 3 seconds?
   - Does it match the audience's awareness level?

2. CONGRUENCE — Does the creative match the landing page?
   - Visual match (colors, imagery, style)
   - Message match (promise, offer, tone)
   - Expectation match (what they clicked for = what they get)

3. CONTINUITY — Is there a consistent thread through the funnel?
   - Ad → Landing Page → Thank You → Email: same story
   - Each step builds on the previous
   - No jarring transitions or mixed messages
```

### Creative Lifecycle Analysis
```
PHASE 1: Learning (Days 1-3)
- Low, unstable performance
- Platform exploring audiences
- DON'T judge yet

PHASE 2: Growth (Days 4-14)
- Performance improving
- Finding best audiences
- SCALE carefully

PHASE 3: Peak (Days 14-30)
- Best performance
- Stable metrics
- MAXIMIZE spend here

PHASE 4: Fatigue (Days 30+)
- Declining performance
- Frequency increasing
- CTR dropping
- PREPARE replacement

SIGNALS OF FATIGUE:
- CTR drops 20%+ from peak
- Frequency > 3 in 7 days
- CPM increasing without external factors
- Engagement rate declining
```
