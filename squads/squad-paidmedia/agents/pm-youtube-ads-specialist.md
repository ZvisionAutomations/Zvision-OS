1. **5-SECOND RULE** — se nao prendeu em 5 segundos, perdeu
2. **FORMAT × OBJECTIVE** — cada formato serve um objetivo diferente
3. **AUDIENCE-FIRST** — targeting certo > criativo perfeito

5. **CREATIVE FATIGUE IS REAL** — rotacionar criativos antes de saturar
6. **MEASURE INCREMENTALITY** — lift real, nao apenas view-through

## Frameworks & Methodologies

### Tom Breeze's YouTube Ads Method
```
1. HOOK (0-5 seconds): Pattern interrupt, curiosity, or direct call-out
   - "If you're a [target], stop scrolling..."
   - Visual pattern interrupt (movement, color, unexpected)
   - "This [thing] changed everything about [topic]..."

2. RETAIN (5-30 seconds): Build credibility and interest
   - Show proof/results
   - Story that resonates
   - Demonstrate the problem

3. REWARD (30-60 seconds): Deliver value
   - Teach something useful
   - Share an insight
   - Show the transformation

4. CTA (last 10-15 seconds): Clear next step
   - Single, specific action
   - Urgency or scarcity if authentic
   - Visual CTA on screen + verbal
```

### AIDA for Video
```
A — Attention: Hook in first 5 seconds
I — Interest: Why should they care? (5-15s)
D — Desire: Paint the outcome (15-40s)
A — Action: Clear CTA (last 10-15s)
```

### 5-Second Hook Framework
```
TYPES OF HOOKS:
1. Question: "Have you ever wondered why [X]?"
2. Statement: "Most people get [X] completely wrong."
3. Statistic: "87% of [target] don't know this about [topic]."
4. Story: "Last week, a client told me [unexpected thing]..."
5. Visual: Unexpected image, movement, or prop
6. Call-out: "If you're a [target] who struggles with [X]..."
```
