# Traffic Masters — Elite Frameworks Reference

Advanced paid media frameworks from elite practitioners.

## Depesh Mandalia — BPM Method (Brand-driven Performance Marketing)
**Source:** SM Commerce, BPM Method training
- **AC-4 Score:** Product (1-5) + Audience (1-5) + Offer (1-5) + Funnel (1-5) = 17+ to scale
- **5W Avatar:** Who, What, Transformation, Functional Why, Why Not — complete in 20 min
- **Graduation Testing (GT):** GT1 (test audiences) → GT2 (test creatives) → GT3 (optimize prospecting)
- **Audience Architecture:** Cold → Warm → Hot → Customer (each tier EXCLUDES the tier below)
- **CBO Cookbook:** Testing → Prospecting → V-Scale → Nitro V-Scale → H-Scale → DCO Infinity → DPA Infinity
- **Bidding Garage:** Cost Cap (Honda/reliable), Min ROAS (slowest growth), Bid Cap (Maserati/risky), Accelerated (Ferrari/highest risk)

## Molly Pittman — Customer Value Journey (CVJ)
**Source:** DigitalMarketer
8 stages: Aware → Engage → Subscribe → Convert → Excite → Ascend → Advocate → Promote
- Map ad campaigns to EACH stage, not just "convert"
- Each stage has specific KPIs and creative types

## Ralph Burns — Tier 11 Method
**Source:** Tier 11 / Perpetual Traffic podcast
- **3C Creative Framework:** Concept × Congruence × Continuity
- Full-funnel creative mapping: awareness (education) → consideration (social proof) → conversion (offer)
- Dynamic Creative Testing at scale

## Kasim Aslam — Solutions 8 Google Ads Method
**Source:** Solutions 8
- **Performance Max Structure:** Asset group per theme, not per product
- **Alpha/Beta Campaign Structure:** Alpha (exact match winners), Beta (broad match exploration)
- **Negative Keyword Sculpting:** systematic exclusion to force traffic to right campaigns

## Pedro Sobral — Sobral Method (BR)
**Source:** Comunidade Sobral
- **Estrutura de Campanhas:** Campanha de Conversão → Campanha de Remarketing → Campanha de Lookalike
- **Regra do 1-3-5:** 1 campanha, 3 conjuntos, 5 anúncios por conjunto
- **Orçamento Mínimo:** R$ 10-20/dia por conjunto para otimização do algoritmo
- **Método de Escala:** Duplicar conjuntos vencedores com +20% orçamento a cada 3 dias
