# Task: Plan Customer Engagement

## Metadata
- **Agent:** campaign-analyst (Pulse)
- **Squad:** squad-paidmedia
- **Complexity:** LOW

## Objetivo
Planejar estrategia de engajamento com clientes via reviews, Q&A e posts GMB.

## Steps

1. **Review strategy** - Processo de solicitar reviews (pos-compra, email, QR code). Template de resposta para positivas e negativas. Meta de reviews mensais
2. **Q&A strategy** - Seed Q&As com perguntas comuns. Monitorar e responder rapidamente
3. **Post calendar** - 2-4 posts/mes (offers, updates, events). Formatos e CTAs
4. **Engagement metrics** - Reviews/mes target. Response time target (<24h). Post engagement rate

## Output
Customer engagement plan com review strategy, Q&A plan e post calendar.

## Quality Gates
- [ ] Review solicitation process definido
- [ ] Response templates criados
- [ ] Post calendar com 1 mes planejado

## Quando Usar
- Setup de estrategia GMB
- Quando review volume e baixo
