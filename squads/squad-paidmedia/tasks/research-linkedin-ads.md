# Task: Research LinkedIn Ads

## Metadata
- **Agent:** creative-strategist (Canvas)
- **Squad:** squad-paidmedia
- **Complexity:** LOW

## Objetivo
Pesquisar campaigns de concorrentes no LinkedIn para intelligence de B2B ad strategy.

## Steps

1. **Pesquisar via LinkedIn company page** - Ir na company page → Ads tab
2. **Catalogar formatos** - Sponsored content (single image, carousel, video). Message ads. Lead gen forms. Text ads
3. **Analisar messaging B2B** - Value propositions. Social proof (logos, stats). Lead magnet offers. CTA patterns
4. **Identificar oportunidades** - Formatos sub-utilizados por concorrentes. Messaging angles inexplorados. Audience targeting gaps

## Output
LinkedIn ad research com catalogacao e insights B2B.

## Quality Gates
- [ ] Min 3 concorrentes pesquisados
- [ ] Formatos e messaging catalogados
- [ ] Oportunidades identificadas
