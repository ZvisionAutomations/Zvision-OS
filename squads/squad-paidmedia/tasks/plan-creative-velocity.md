# Task: Plan Creative Velocity

## Metadata
- **Agent:** creative-strategist (Canvas)
- **Squad:** squad-paidmedia
- **Complexity:** MEDIUM

## Objetivo
Planejar cadencia de producao de criativos baseada no spend level e fase de scaling.

## Steps

1. **Determinar velocity necessaria**
   | Spend/day | Criativos/semana | Formatos |
   |-----------|-----------------|----------|
   | <$500 | 1-2 | Image + Video |
   | $500-$2K | 2-4 | Image + Video + Carousel |
   | $2K-$10K | 4-8 | All formats + UGC |
   | $10K-$50K | 8-12 | All + Dynamic + Iterations |
   | $50K+ | 12+ | Full production pipeline |

2. **Definir production pipeline**
   - Conceito: 1-2 dias (Canvas)
   - Producao: 2-3 dias (design/video team)
   - Review: 1 dia (Canvas + stakeholder)
   - Upload + config: 1 dia (Signal/Query)
   - Total cycle: 5-7 dias

3. **Definir mix de producao**
   - 60% iteracoes de winners (novas versoes de criativos que performam)
   - 30% novos conceitos (testar angles novos)
   - 10% experimental (formatos ou approaches radicalmente diferentes)

4. **Planejar recursos**
   - Quantos designers/editores necessarios por velocity level
   - Templates reutilizaveis para acelerar producao
   - AI tools para gerar variacoes (Midjourney, Runway, etc.)
   - UGC creators (se aplicavel)

5. **Definir calendar**
   - Sprint semanal de creative com deadlines
   - Buffer de 1 semana de criativos prontos (runway)
   - Alerta quando runway <3 dias

## Output
Creative velocity plan com cadencia, pipeline, mix e calendar.

## Quality Gates
- [ ] Velocity alinhada com spend level
- [ ] Pipeline com timeline realista
- [ ] Mix definido (iteracoes/novos/experimental)
- [ ] Calendar com deadlines semanais

## Quando Usar
- Setup de producao criativa para conta
- Scaling de budget (precisa mais criativos)
- Quando creative fatigue e constante
