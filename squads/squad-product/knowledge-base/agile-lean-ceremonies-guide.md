# Agile & Lean Ceremonies Guide

## Purpose
Guia pratico de todas as ceremonias ageis utilizadas pela squad, com formato, duracao, participantes e output esperado.

## Sprint Ceremonies

### Sprint Planning
- **Frequency:** Start of each sprint
- **Duration:** 2-3 hours (2-week sprint)
- **Participants:** Full team + PO
- **Input:** Prioritized backlog, capacity plan
- **Output:** Sprint goal, sprint backlog, commitment
- **Key Rule:** Confidence vote >= 3.5/5 before committing

### Daily Standup
- **Frequency:** Daily
- **Duration:** 15 min max
- **Participants:** Dev team + PM
- **Format:** "What I did / What I will do / Blockers"
- **Key Rule:** Updates, not discussions. Park topics for after.

### Sprint Review (Demo)
- **Frequency:** End of each sprint
- **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Participants:** Full team + client stakeholders
- **Format:** Demo working software, collect feedback
- **Key Rule:** Show outcomes, not code

### Sprint Retrospective
- **Frequency:** After sprint review
- **Duration:** 1-1.5 hours
- **Participants:** Dev team (client optional)
- **Formats:** 4Ls, Start/Stop/Continue, Sailboat, Mad/Sad/Glad
- **Output:** Max 3 action items with owners + deadlines
- **Key Rule:** Rotate format, max 3 actions, accountability check

### Backlog Refinement
- **Frequency:** Mid-sprint (1x per sprint)
- **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Participants:** PM + Dev lead + QA
- **Purpose:** Clarify, estimate, and prepare top backlog items
- **Output:** Items "sprint ready" (clear AC, estimated, dependencies resolved)
- **Key Rule:** Refine 2 sprints ahead

## Product Ceremonies

### Weekly Discovery Sync
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Duration:** 30 min
- **Participants:** Product Trio (PM + Design + Eng)
- **Purpose:** Share interview insights, update OST, decide next tests
- **Key Rule:** No week without user contact

### Monthly Metrics Review
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Duration:** 30-45 min
- **Participants:** PM + Analyst + Stakeholders
- **Purpose:** Review product metrics, trends, health score
- **Output:** Metrics report with narrative and actions

### Quarterly Planning
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Duration:** Half-day
- **Participants:** PM + Client leadership + Agency leadership
- **Purpose:** OKR review, roadmap update, strategy alignment
- **Output:** Next quarter OKRs, updated roadmap, aligned priorities

## Client Ceremonies

### Weekly Client Sync
- **Duration:** 30 min
- **Participants:** PM + Client PO
- **Purpose:** Progress update, decisions, priority alignment
- **Format:** Blockers → Decisions → Progress → Next week

### QBR (Quarterly Business Review)
- **Duration:** 90-120 min
- **Participants:** PM + Client leadership
- **Purpose:** Value demonstration, strategy alignment, planning
- **Format:** Review → Challenges → Strategy → Next Quarter
