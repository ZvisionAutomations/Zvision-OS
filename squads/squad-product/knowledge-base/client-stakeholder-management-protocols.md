# Client & Stakeholder Management Protocols

## Purpose
Protocolos de gestao de stakeholders e clientes para agencia, incluindo comunicacao, escalacao e gestao de expectativas.

## Stakeholder Mapping (Power/Interest Grid)
```
HIGH POWER │ MANAGE CLOSELY  │ KEEP SATISFIED
           │ (Key Players)    │ (Context Setters)
           │ Regular meetings │ Periodic updates
           ├──────────────────┼──────────────────
           │ KEEP INFORMED    │ MONITOR
LOW POWER  │ (Subjects)       │ (Crowd)
           │ Status updates   │ General comms
           └──────────────────┴──────────────────
           HIGH INTEREST       LOW INTEREST
```

## Communication Cadence Matrix
| Stakeholder Type | Channel | Frequency | Content | Owner |
|-----------------|---------|-----------|---------|-------|
| Executive Sponsor | Email + QBR | Monthly + Quarterly | Strategic updates, ROI | Client PM |
| Client PO | Slack + Weekly sync | Daily async + Weekly | Decisions, priorities | Client PM |
| Technical Lead | Standup | Daily | Implementation details | Delivery Mgr |
| End Users | In-product + Survey | Quarterly | Feature updates, feedback | Discovery Lead |
| Finance | Email | Monthly | Budget utilization | Client PM |

## Escalation Protocol
| Level | Trigger | Response Time | Responder |
|-------|---------|-------------|-----------|
| L1 | Communication issue, minor delay | Same day | PM |
| L2 | Budget concern, priority disagreement | 24h | PM + Lead |
| L3 | Contract dispute, strategic misalignment | 48h | Leadership |
| L4 | At-risk account, potential churn | Immediate | Executive |

## Difficult Conversation Templates

### Delivering Bad News
"I want to share an update on [topic]. We encountered [specific issue]. The impact is [clear description]. We are addressing it by [action plan]. We expect resolution by [timeline]. I want to discuss [any decisions needed from you]."

### Pushing Back on Scope
"I understand this is important because [their reason]. Let me share what this would involve: [impact]. I see three options: [Option A], [Option B], [Option C]. I recommend [option] because [rationale]. What would you prefer?"

### Requesting Client Action
"To keep [project] on track, we need [specific deliverable] by [date]. This is blocking [what it blocks]. If delayed, the impact would be [consequence]. Can you commit to this timeline?"

## Client Health Indicators
| Signal | Health | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Responds quickly, engaged in meetings | Healthy | Maintain |
| Slow responses, cancels meetings | Attention | Proactive check-in |
| Complaints, negative feedback | At Risk | Recovery plan |
| Champion leaves organization | Critical | Relationship rebuild |
| Requests data export | Churn Risk | Executive escalation |
