# Discovery Readiness Checklist

## Purpose
Verificar se todas as pre-condicoes para iniciar um sprint de discovery estao atendidas.

## Pre-Discovery Gate
- [ ] **Scope defined:** Research questions documented and approved
- [ ] **Trigger clear:** Business reason for discovery articulated
- [ ] **Decision identified:** What decision depends on this research?
- [ ] **Methods selected:** 2+ research methods chosen with rationale
- [ ] **Team assigned:** Product Trio (PM + Design + Eng) confirmed
- [ ] **Participants recruited:** 5-8 participants per segment confirmed/scheduled
- [ ] **Tools ready:** Interview guide, prototype (if needed), recording setup
- [ ] **Timeline set:** Sprint duration defined with milestones
- [ ] **Success criteria:** What evidence validates/invalidates defined BEFORE starting
- [ ] **Budget approved:** Time allocation confirmed for discovery sprint
- [ ] **Stakeholders informed:** Key stakeholders know discovery is happening
- [ ] **Previous research reviewed:** Checked for existing relevant findings

## Quality Gate
**Pass criteria:** All 12 items checked
**If items missing:** Address gaps before starting fieldwork (desk research can begin)
