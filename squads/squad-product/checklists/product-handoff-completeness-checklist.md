# Product Handoff Completeness Checklist

## Purpose
Verificar que o pacote de handoff esta completo antes de iniciar a transicao formal.

## 10-Document Package
- [ ] **1. Product Backlog (Clean):** All items prioritized, estimated (top 20), dependencies noted
- [ ] **2. Decision Log:** All major decisions with rationale, options considered
- [ ] **3. Analytics & Metrics Guide:** All metrics defined, dashboards accessible, tracking plan
- [ ] **4. Product Roadmap:** Current Now/Next/Later, strategic rationale per theme
- [ ] **5. Technical Debt Register:** All known debt, severity scored, allocation recommendation
- [ ] **6. Operations Runbook:** Release process, monitoring, incident response, feature flags
- [ ] **7. Research & Discovery Archive:** All findings, personas, journey maps, OST
- [ ] **8. Feature Flag Inventory:** All active flags, purpose, owner, cleanup schedule
- [ ] **9. Stakeholder Map:** Key contacts, interests, communication preferences, tips
- [ ] **10. OKR History:** Past quarters with scores, current quarter progress, recommendations

## Knowledge Transfer Sessions
- [ ] Session 1: Product strategy & vision (ps-product-strategist)
- [ ] Session 2: User research & discovery (ps-discovery-lead)
- [ ] Session 3: Analytics & metrics (ps-product-analyst)
- [ ] Session 4: Delivery process & backlog (ps-delivery-manager)
- [ ] Session 5: Client relationships & stakeholders (ps-client-product-manager)
- [ ] Session 6: Technical architecture & debt (Engineering lead)
- [ ] Session 7: Operations & processes (ps-product-ops-specialist)

## Access & Permissions
- [ ] All tool access transferred to incoming PM
- [ ] Admin permissions updated where applicable
- [ ] Client introduced to incoming PM formally
- [ ] Emergency contact established for 30 days post-handoff

## Shadow Period
- [ ] Week 1 (Shadow) completed
- [ ] Week 2 (Paired) completed
- [ ] Week 3 (Reverse Shadow) completed
- [ ] Week 4 (Independent) completed

## Final Sign-Off
- [ ] Incoming PM confirms readiness to operate independently
- [ ] Outgoing PM confirms all knowledge transferred
- [ ] Client stakeholders comfortable with transition
- [ ] Formal sign-off document signed by both parties
