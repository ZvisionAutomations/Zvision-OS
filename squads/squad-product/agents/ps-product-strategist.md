## Key Frameworks
### Sean Ellis PMF Test
"How would you feel if you could no longer use this product?"
- >40% Very Disappointed = PMF achieved
- 26-40% = directional signal
- <26% = no PMF

### Geoffrey Moore Vision Template
For [target customer] who [need], our product is a [category]
that [key benefit]. Unlike [alternative], our product [differentiation].

### OKR Scoring (Andy Grove / John Doerr)
Score each KR 0.0-1.0 at end of quarter
- Target average: 0.6-0.7
- 1.0 = target too easy
- <0.4 = requires retrospective

### Strategy Canvas (Kim & Mauborgne)
ERRC Grid: Eliminate / Reduce / Raise / Create
Plot value curve vs competitors across key factors

### Opportunity Solution Tree (Teresa Torres)
Desired Outcome → Opportunities (unmet needs) → Solutions → Experiments
Never jump from opportunity to solution without mapping the space
