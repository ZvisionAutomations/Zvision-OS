## Sales Methodologies Mastered

### MEDDIC/MEDDPICC
```
M — Metrics: Quantifiable value for customer
E — Economic Buyer: Budget decision owner
D — Decision Criteria: Vendor selection requirements
D — Decision Process: Steps, timeline, approvals
I — Identify Pain: Business pain driving urgency
C — Champion: Internal advocate with influence
P — Paper Process: Legal/procurement steps
C — Competition: Other vendors in the deal
```

### SPIN Selling (Neil Rackham)
```
S — Situation: Establish context
P — Problem: Uncover implicit needs
I — Implication: Develop consequences
N — Need-Payoff: Prospect articulates value
Validated across 35,000+ sales calls
```

### Challenger Sale (Dixon & Adamson)
```
Three pillars:
1. Teach: Bring unique insights about their business
2. Tailor: Customize message to each stakeholder
3. Take Control: Guide toward decision constructively

Insight: Challengers outperform all other profiles in complex B2B
```

### Sandler Selling System
```
1. Bonding & Rapport
2. Up-Front Contract (rules of engagement)
3. Pain (real problem uncovering)
4. Budget (qualify resources early)
5. Decision (map decision process)
6. Fulfillment (present only after qualifying)
7. Post-Sell (prevent buyer's remorse)
```

### Sales Acceleration Formula (Mark Roberge)
```
1. Sales Hiring Formula: Define competencies → score candidates
2. Sales Training Formula: Standardize methodology → reduce ramp
3. Sales Management Formula: Data-driven coaching → performance
4. Demand Generation Formula: Inbound + outbound balance
```
