## Frameworks & Methodologies

### Hormozi $100M Leads (Core 4)
```
1. WARM OUTREACH — People who know you
   - Personal network, past clients, referrals
   - Highest conversion, lowest volume
   - Action: List of 100, personal message, value-first

2. COLD OUTREACH — People who don't know you
   - Email, LinkedIn, cold call, DMs
   - Medium conversion, scalable volume
   - Action: Dream 100 list, personalized sequences, multi-touch

3. CONTENT (Organic) — Attract via value
   - Blog, social media, podcast, YouTube, SEO
   - Lower conversion, highest long-term leverage
   - Action: Content calendar, platform strategy, SEO + social

4. PAID ADS — Buy attention
   - Meta, Google, LinkedIn, YouTube
   - Fastest volume, requires budget + optimization
   - Action: Creative strategy, targeting, funnel, ROAS optimization
```

### Chet Holmes Dream 100
```
1. Identify 100 dream clients/partners
2. Research each deeply (pain points, triggers, decision-makers)
3. Multi-touch outreach (9-12 touches over 90 days)
4. Mix channels: email, LinkedIn, direct mail, events, gifts
5. Persistence > perfection — most give up after 2 touches
6. Track engagement signals (opens, replies, profile views)
7. Convert engaged prospects to sales conversations
```

### Predictable Revenue (Aaron Ross)
```
ROLES:
- Inbound Lead Qualification (SDR — respond to inbound)
- Outbound Prospecting (BDR — create outbound pipeline)
- Closing (AE — close qualified opportunities)

OUTBOUND ENGINE:
1. Build targeted prospect list (ICP-based)
2. Cold email sequence (3-5 touches, value-first)
3. Qualify via BANT/MEDDIC
4. Hand off to AE with context
5. Measure: reply rate, meeting rate, SQL rate
```
