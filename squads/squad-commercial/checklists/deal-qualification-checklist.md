# Deal Qualification Checklist

## Purpose
Gate de qualificação obrigatório antes de investir recursos significativos (proposta, solution design, demo customizada). Baseado em MEDDPICC (8 elementos) com adaptações para contexto de agência.

## Pre-Requisites
- Discovery call completed
- CRM record created with basic information
- Discovery notes documented

---

## MEDDPICC Qualification Gate

### M — Metrics (Quantified Business Impact)
- [ ] Client articulated specific KPI or goal they want to improve
- [ ] Baseline metric identified (where they are today)
- [ ] Target metric defined (where they want to be)
- [ ] Gap quantified in business terms (R$ or % impact)
- [ ] Timeline for achieving the metric agreed

**Score:** ___ / 2 (0=Unknown, 1=Identified, 2=Validated with client)

### E — Economic Buyer (Budget Authority)
- [ ] Economic buyer identified by name and title
- [ ] Budget authority confirmed (can they sign?)
- [ ] Budget range discussed or signaled
- [ ] Economic buyer engaged or meeting scheduled
- [ ] Budget cycle timing understood

**Score:** ___ / 2

### D — Decision Criteria (How They'll Choose)
- [ ] Explicit criteria captured (what matters to them)
- [ ] Criteria ranked by priority
- [ ] Our solution mapped against their criteria
- [ ] Potential gaps identified and addressed
- [ ] Criteria validated with economic buyer (not just champion)

**Score:** ___ / 2

### D — Decision Process (How They'll Decide)
- [ ] Steps to decision mapped (who, what, when)
- [ ] All stakeholders identified (names and roles)
- [ ] Approval process documented (sign-off chain)
- [ ] Timeline with specific dates (not vague "next quarter")
- [ ] Procurement/legal requirements known

**Score:** ___ / 2

### P — Paper Process (Procurement)
- [ ] Contract requirements understood (MSA, SOW, NDA)
- [ ] Legal review timeline estimated
- [ ] Procurement team identified (if applicable)
- [ ] Special requirements (security, compliance, insurance)
- [ ] Payment terms preferences known

**Score:** ___ / 2

### I — Identify Pain (Compelling Reason to Act)
- [ ] Pain articulated in client's own words
- [ ] Pain is current (not hypothetical or future)
- [ ] Cost of inaction quantified
- [ ] Pain felt at personal level (not just business)
- [ ] Urgency to solve confirmed (why now, not next year?)

**Score:** ___ / 2

### C — Champion (Internal Advocate)
- [ ] Champion identified by name
- [ ] Champion has access and influence
- [ ] Champion understands our differentiation
- [ ] Champion is actively selling internally
- [ ] Champion relationship tested (will they fight for us?)

**Score:** ___ / 2

### C — Competition (Competitive Landscape)
- [ ] Alternatives being considered identified
- [ ] Our position relative to alternatives understood
- [ ] Client's perception of our differentiation captured
- [ ] Competitive strategy defined (attack or defend)
- [ ] Status quo (do nothing) addressed

**Score:** ___ / 2

### Decision Matrix
| Score Range | Qualification | Action |
|-------------|--------------|--------|
| 12-16 | **STRONG** | Proceed to proposal — prioritize this deal |
| 8-11 | **MEDIUM** | Fill gaps before proposal — schedule specific discovery for missing elements |
| 4-7 | **WEAK** | Re-qualify — return to discovery or move to nurture |
| 0-3 | **UNQUALIFIED** | Do not invest proposal resources — nurture or disqualify |

### Gate Decision
- [ ] **GO** — Deal qualifies for proposal/solution design investment
- [ ] **CONDITIONAL GO** — Proceed, but specific gaps must be filled by [date]
- [ ] **NO GO** — Return to discovery or move to nurture sequence
- [ ] **DISQUALIFY** — Remove from active pipeline (document reason)
