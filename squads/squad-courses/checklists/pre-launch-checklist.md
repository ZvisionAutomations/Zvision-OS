# Pre-Launch Checklist

Complete all items in this checklist before announcing or opening enrollment. No item is optional.

---

## Platform Readiness

- [ ] Course is published on platform (not in draft mode)
- [ ] Course title, description, and thumbnail are correct
- [ ] All modules and lessons are in correct order
- [ ] Course access type confirmed (open enrollment / drip / cohort start date)
- [ ] Student view tested — all content loads correctly
- [ ] Mobile view tested — content is accessible and readable
- [ ] Platform support channel is configured and monitored

---

## Content Uploaded

- [ ] All video lessons uploaded and playable
- [ ] All written lessons published
- [ ] All slides/presentations uploaded or linked
- [ ] All workbooks and PDFs attached as downloads
- [ ] All downloads open correctly (not corrupt or broken)
- [ ] Quizzes configured and functional on platform
- [ ] Final project brief published and accessible

---

## Pricing and Checkout

- [ ] Pricing tier(s) configured correctly on platform
- [ ] Payment methods enabled (credit card, PIX, PayPal — as applicable)
- [ ] Installment options configured (if applicable)
- [ ] Discount code tested (if launch uses one)
- [ ] Test purchase completed successfully
- [ ] Access granted immediately after test purchase
- [ ] Enrollment confirmation email received after test purchase
- [ ] Invoice/receipt email received and correct

---

## Sales Page

- [ ] Sales page is live and publicly accessible (not behind login)
- [ ] Headline clearly states the transformation
- [ ] Module overview matches actual course content
- [ ] Pricing shown is correct and matches checkout
- [ ] All CTA buttons link to correct checkout page
- [ ] No broken links on the page
- [ ] Page loads in under 3 seconds
- [ ] Mobile layout reviewed and acceptable
- [ ] Testimonials section populated (or placeholder removed)
- [ ] FAQ addresses the top 5–8 student objections

---

## Email Sequences

- [ ] Welcome email configured and tested (trigger: enrollment)
- [ ] Welcome email contains correct links (platform, community, support)
- [ ] Onboarding sequence (3–5 emails) configured and scheduled
- [ ] All email links tested and functional
- [ ] Unsubscribe link works correctly
- [ ] Sender name and email address are correct
- [ ] Pre-launch/launch emails scheduled (if applicable)

---

## Enrollment Funnel

- [ ] Enrollment funnel tested end-to-end (opt-in → email → sales page → checkout → enrolled)
- [ ] Conversion tracking configured (UTM, pixel, or analytics goal)
- [ ] Affiliate links configured (if affiliate launch)
- [ ] Cart abandonment sequence configured (if applicable)

---

## Student Onboarding

- [ ] Welcome video or message from instructor is configured
- [ ] Community access instructions sent in welcome email
- [ ] Student knows where to start (start here lesson or pinned message)
- [ ] Onboarding experience tested as a new student
- [ ] First module accessible and all content loads
- [ ] Progress tracking works (completion registers when lesson is finished)

---

## Support Readiness

- [ ] FAQ page or doc exists and is linked in welcome email
- [ ] Support email or form is configured and monitored
- [ ] Community moderation plan in place
- [ ] Instructor availability communicated to students (response time expectations)

---

## Legal and Compliance

- [ ] Terms of purchase / refund policy linked on checkout page
- [ ] Privacy policy linked (if collecting email addresses)
- [ ] Course access duration clearly communicated (lifetime / 1 year / cohort window)
