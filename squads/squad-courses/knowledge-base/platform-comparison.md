# Course Platform Comparison

Decision reference for selecting the right platform for each course type and business model.

---

## Platform Overview Table

| Platform | Best For | Pricing Model | Transaction Fee | Audience Built-In |
|----------|----------|--------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Hotmart** | Brazilian/LATAM market | Free + % of sales | 9.9% + $0.50 (standard) | Yes — marketplace |
| **Kajabi** | Full business (course + email + community) | $149–$399/mo | None | No |
| **Teachable** | Simple video courses, beginner-friendly | Free–$299/mo | 0–5% | No |
| **Udemy** | Volume at low price, no existing audience | Free (rev share) | 37–75% to platform | Yes — massive |
| **Thinkific** | Growth-stage creator, community optional | Free–$199/mo | None on paid plans | No |

---

## Hotmart

**Audience:** Brazil, Portugal, LATAM (strong affiliate ecosystem)

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Payment | Credit card, PIX, boleto, international |
| Affiliate system | Built-in, strong community of affiliates |
| Community | Basic (Hotmart Club) |
| Email marketing | Limited — integrate with ActiveCampaign/Mailchimp |
| Video hosting | Included |
| Mobile app | Yes |

**Pros:** Largest LATAM course marketplace, affiliate traffic, PIX support
**Cons:** Transaction fees add up, limited design control, outdated UI

**Best for:** Courses in Portuguese, affiliate-driven launches, LATAM audience

---

## Kajabi

**Audience:** English-speaking market, premium positioning

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Payment | Stripe, PayPal |
| Affiliate system | Built-in |
| Community | Full community platform included |
| Email marketing | Full-featured built-in |
| Video hosting | Included |
| Mobile app | Yes (white-label) |

**Pros:** All-in-one (no Mailchimp, no separate community), beautiful design, strong analytics
**Cons:** Expensive ($149+/mo), English-centric, overkill for small catalogs

**Best for:** Full creator businesses, email-first strategy, English market

---

## Teachable

**Audience:** Creators with own audience, beginner-friendly setup

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Payment | Stripe, PayPal, Teachable Pay |
| Affiliate system | Available (Pro+) |
| Community | Not native — integrate with Circle |
| Email marketing | Basic (use external) |
| Video hosting | Included |
| Mobile app | Yes |

**Pros:** Simple setup, good UX for students, reasonable pricing
**Cons:** Community not built-in, transaction fees on free plan, limited customization

**Best for:** First course, simple video course, small catalog

---

## Udemy

**Audience:** Learners browsing the marketplace (not your list)

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Payment | Platform handles all |
| Affiliate system | Yes (Udemy affiliates + your own) |
| Community | Q&A only |
| Email marketing | Very limited — platform-controlled |
| Video hosting | Included |
| Mobile app | Yes (very polished) |

**Pros:** Massive built-in traffic, no hosting costs, handles everything
**Cons:** Race to bottom on pricing (frequent $10 sales), no email list ownership, platform controls pricing

**Best for:** Volume play, brand exposure, international reach at low price

---

## Thinkific

**Audience:** Mid-market creators, team training, B2B

| Feature | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Payment | Stripe, PayPal |
| Affiliate system | Available |
| Community | Thinkific Communities (add-on) |
| Email marketing | Basic (use external) |
| Video hosting | Included |
| Mobile app | Yes |

**Pros:** No transaction fees on paid plans, solid analytics, B2B features
**Cons:** Less polish than Kajabi, community is add-on, smaller ecosystem

**Best for:** Growing creator with own audience, corporate/B2B training

---

## Decision Guide

| Situation | Recommended Platform |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Brazilian/LATAM market | Hotmart |
| Full creator business (email + community) | Kajabi |
| First course, simple setup | Teachable |
| No existing audience, want marketplace traffic | Udemy |
| B2B / corporate training | Thinkific |
| Already have email list (1k+) | Kajabi or Thinkific |
| Launch with affiliates in LATAM | Hotmart |
