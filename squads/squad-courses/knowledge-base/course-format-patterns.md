# Course Format Patterns

Reference for selecting and structuring each course delivery format.

---

## Format Selection Matrix

| Format | Best For | Typical Duration | Price Range | Completion Rate |
|--------|----------|-----------------|-------------|-----------------|
| Video Course | Self-paced skill building | 2–10 hours | $97–$997 | 15–30% |
| Written Course | Text-heavy topics, frameworks | 1–5 hours reading | $47–$497 | 25–40% |
| Workshop | Intensive skill application | 3–8 hours live | $97–$2,000 | 85–95% |
| Mentoring Presentation | Internal team training, pitches | 30–90 min | N/A (internal) | ~100% |
| Mini-Course | Lead magnet, entry product | 30–90 min | $0–$97 | 40–60% |
| Cohort-Based | Community + accountability | 4–12 weeks | $500–$5,000 | 60–80% |

---

## Video Course

**Components:**
- Welcome video (2–3 min)
- Modules (3–8) with 3–6 video lessons each
- Lesson length: 5–15 min optimal
- Workbook or PDF per module
- Quizzes or exercises at module end
- Final project or capstone

**Structure per lesson:**
1. Hook (30 sec) — what and why
2. Content (main delivery)
3. Summary (30 sec)
4. Action item or exercise

**Production needs:** Camera or screen recording, audio, editing, platform

---

## Written Course

**Components:**
- Text-based lessons (1,000–3,000 words each)
- Embedded exercises and reflection prompts
- Downloadable templates or workbooks
- Optional: short video walkthroughs per module

**Structure per lesson:**
1. Objective statement
2. Concept explanation
3. Example or case study
4. Exercise or application
5. Key takeaways

**Platform:** Notion, Gumroad (PDF), Kajabi, Teachable (text blocks)

---

## Workshop

**Components:**
- Pre-workshop prep material (optional)
- Opening: context + outcomes (15 min)
- Content blocks of 20–30 min max
- Breakouts or exercises every 30 min
- Q&A throughout
- Closing: action plan + resources

**Timing rule:** No content block longer than 30 minutes without interaction.

**Facilitation tools:** Slides, Miro/Figma board, breakout rooms, polls, chat

---

## Mentoring Presentation

**Components:**
- Context slide (why this matters)
- Framework or model (the core idea)
- 2–3 application examples
- Discussion or Q&A
- Next steps / resources

**Duration:** 30–60 min including discussion
**Tone:** Collaborative, not lecture. Invite reactions, not just questions.
**Output:** Slide deck + follow-up resource doc

---

## Mini-Course

**Components:**
- 3–7 short lessons (5–10 min each)
- One transformation or quick win
- Optional: bonus lesson or upgrade offer

**Goal:** Demonstrate value, build trust, generate leads or upsells.
**Delivery:** Email sequence, platform module, or short PDF series

---

## Cohort-Based Course (CBC)

**Components:**
- Weekly live sessions (60–90 min)
- Async lessons or reading between sessions
- Community space (Slack, Circle, Discord)
- Group assignments + peer review
- Office hours or 1:1 touchpoints
- Final project + showcase or graduation

**Cohort size:** 10–50 students optimal
**Instructor time:** 5–15 hours/week during cohort

**Differentiators:**
- Accountability through peers
- Live feedback from instructor
- Community as product feature
- Fixed timeline creates urgency

---

## Format Stacking (Ladder)

A common product ladder:

```
Mini-Course (free/low-ticket) → Video Course (mid-ticket) → Cohort-Based (high-ticket)
```

Each level delivers more transformation and demands more from the student and instructor.
