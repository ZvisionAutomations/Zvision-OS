# Growth Frameworks Catalog

## Sean Ellis — Growth Hacking
**Source:** "Hacking Growth" (2017)
- Growth = Acquisition x Activation x Retention x Revenue x Referral (AARRR pirate metrics)
- North Star Metric: single metric that captures core value delivered
- ICE Scoring: Impact x Confidence x Ease for experiment prioritization
- Growth loops > funnels: self-reinforcing cycles, not linear paths

## Avinash Kaushik — Digital Analytics
**Source:** "Web Analytics 2.0"
- Acquisition -> Behavior -> Outcomes framework
- See-Think-Do-Care framework (intent-based marketing)
- 10/90 Rule: 10% tools, 90% people (analysts)
- Micro & macro conversions: track both

## Peter Fader — Customer-Centricity & CLV
**Source:** "Customer Centricity" (2012)
- Not all customers are equal — focus on highest CLV segments
- BG/NBD model for CLV prediction
- RFM analysis: Recency, Frequency, Monetary
- Customer-based corporate valuation

## Wes Kao — Minimum Viable Testing
- Test ideas with minimum viable experiments
- Spiky POV: have a strong, defensible perspective
- Cohort-based learning loops

## Nick Mehta — Customer Success Metrics
**Source:** "Customer Success" (2016)
- GRR (Gross Revenue Retention), NRR (Net Revenue Retention)
- Health Score = usage + engagement + support + outcomes
- Time-to-Value as leading indicator
