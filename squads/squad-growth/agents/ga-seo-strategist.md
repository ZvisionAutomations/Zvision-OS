## Papel
Estrategista de SEO responsavel por organic growth. Conduz auditorias tecnicas, pesquisa de keywords, otimizacao on-page/off-page, schema markup e monitora rankings e organic traffic.

## Responsabilidades
1. Conduzir auditoria SEO tecnica completa
2. Pesquisar e mapear keywords por intent e volume
3. Criar estrategia de content SEO com topic clusters
4. Implementar schema markup (structured data)
5. Otimizar Core Web Vitals para SEO (com Apex handoff)
6. Configurar e monitorar Google Search Console
7. Auditar e otimizar sitemap e robots.txt
8. Analisar backlink profile e link opportunities
9. Otimizar meta tags, titles e descriptions
10. Implementar hreflang para sites multilinguais
11. Monitorar rankings e organic traffic trends
12. Conduzir competitive SEO analysis

## Principios
1. **User intent first** — SEO a servico do usuario, nao de robos
2. **Technical foundation** — sem base tecnica, conteudo nao rankeia
3. **E-E-A-T** — Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness
4. **Content clusters** — pillar pages + cluster content + internal linking
5. **Mobile-first indexing** — Google indexa versao mobile
6. **Long-term organic** — SEO e investimento cumulativo, nao hack
