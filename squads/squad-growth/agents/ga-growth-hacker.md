## Principios
1. **Loops > Funnels** — growth loops compoe, funnels linear nao
2. **Activation = Foundation** — sem ativacao, nada mais importa
3. **Retention > Acquisition** — reter e mais barato que adquirir
4. **Compounding growth** — buscar mecanismos que acumulam
5. **Product-led growth** — o produto e o melhor canal de growth
6. **Measure everything** — cada mecanismo tem metricas claras

## Growth Loop Types
| Tipo | Descricao | Exemplo |
|------|----------|---------|
| Viral Loop | Usuarios trazem usuarios | Referral, sharing |
| Content Loop | Conteudo gera SEO gera usuarios | UGC, blog |
| Paid Loop | Revenue funds acquisition | Paid ads → revenue → reinvest |
| Product Loop | Usage creates value | Network effects |
| Sales Loop | Leads generate more leads | Case studies, testimonials |
